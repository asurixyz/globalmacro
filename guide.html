OPERATOR'S MANUAL

Clearance Level: Governor

Subject: Mandate Objectives & Protocols

1. The Objective

Your mandate is simple but perilous: Survive a 1200-day term as the central governor of a major economy.

You begin with 50% Reputation. This is your political capital. If it falls to 0%, you will be ousted in a coup. If you survive to Day 1200, you retire a legend.

WARNING: Reputation decays rapidly if:
  • Inflation exceeds 5%
  • Growth turns negative (Recession)
  • Debt-to-GDP exceeds 100%

2. The Controls

You have three primary levers to pull. Use them wisely.

Policy Rate (Interest Rates)

The cost of borrowing money. Raising rates cools down inflation but hurts growth. Lowering rates stimulates growth but risks inflation.

  • Raise Rates: To fight high inflation or defend your currency.
  • Cut Rates: To fight a recession or stimulate demand.

Fiscal Balance

Government spending relative to tax revenue. A deficit (negative number) stimulates the economy but adds to the debt pile.

  • Deficit Spending (-): Boosts growth immediately. Increases debt.
  • Surplus (+): Pays down debt. Drags on growth.

Tariffs

Taxes on imports. High tariffs can improve your trade balance (Net Exports) but may invite retaliation and hurt global trade efficiency.

3. Key Indicators

  • GDP Growth: The heartbeat of your economy. Keep it positive (>2% is ideal).
  • Inflation: The silent killer. Keep it stable between 1% and 3%.
  • Debt / GDP: The burden on future generations. Keep it under 100% to avoid a crisis.
  • FX Rate: The value of your currency. A crash can lead to imported inflation.
PRO TIP: Markets are volatile. Shocks (Oil spikes, Panic) will happen. Do not overreact to every blip. Steady hands win the mandate.
